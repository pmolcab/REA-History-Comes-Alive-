History Comes Alive!

  • Your mission!

In this project, you will travel back in time and become a historical character. Working in pairs, you will investigate the life of an important figure from history and create an interview in English using chroma key technology.

By the end of the project, you will have produced and edited your own video interview, combining historical knowledge, creativity, communication skills and digital competence.

Are you ready to bring history to life?


  • What will you create?

Working in pairs, you will create a video interview in which one student acts as the interviewer and the other becomes a historical character.

Your final video must include:

  • A brief introduction of the character.
  • An interview with at least 6–8 questions and answers.
  • Accurate historical information.
  • Correct use of English.
  • A chroma key background related to the character's life or achievements.
  • Basic video editing.

The final product will be presented to the class.


  • Suggested Historical Characters

Marie Curie
Albert Einstein
Miguel de Cervantes
Cleopatra
Virginia Wolf
Nelson Mandela
William Shakespeare
Jane Austen
Frida Kahlo
Martin Luther King Jr.

You may also choose another historical character with your teacher's approval.

  • Before you start...

Every challenge is an opportunity to learn something new. During this project, you will research, create, perform and edit your own video.

Listen to this song and remember: success comes from effort, perseverance and teamwork.

Believe in yourself, trust your partner and enjoy the journey!

Now, let's bring history to life!
